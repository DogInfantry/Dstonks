It explores the transition from traditional care models to a technology-driven, value-based ecosystem characterized by "Integrated Resilience".
The project focuses on navigating the "confidence gap" where executives feel optimistic about their organizations' financial outlooks despite geopolitical fragmentation, regulatory divergence, and economic volatility.
